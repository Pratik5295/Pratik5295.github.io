A Hell of a Shift

Description

A 2D point-and-click narrative driven, puzzle solving game made using Unity and C#. Play as an intern who freshly joined the game development studio and complete tasks to progress your career. Keep your pressure gauge in check to survive the game or get "fired". This work simulator has a deep secret associated with it, can you figure it out?

Gameplay

Mechanics

  • Pressure System: The health system of the game which is affected by your ability to solve tasks and their accuracy.
  • Drag and Drop: Main mechanic used in all puzzles of the game.
  • Complex UI system: An entire complex system of UI screens working in sync across various systems.
  • Dialogue System: A branched out with options dialogue system disguised in form of email system in game.
  • Advanced Event Bus: An event bus firing different types of events along with a trigger point in game.
  • Data Handling: An ecosystem of scriptable objects to handle all kinds of data ranging from characters, dialogues, tasks, events, puzzles.
